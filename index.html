Gameplay Loop

The game revolves around a central cycle of risk and reward. This interactive flowchart shows how players will progress, making choices that directly impact their social standing and the challenges they face.

1. Get StuffEarn cash, find dealers
2. Go to EventsParties, hangouts
3. Get PopularDo dares, share drugs
4. Stay HiddenPass "Sober Tests"
5. Face ResultsGrounded or new friends
6. RepeatClimb the social ladder
