New Comer

Overview Gameplay Systems

Survive High School. Throw the Party.

A social survival sim where you navigate the treacherous halls of Northwood High, build your crew, and aim for legendary status.

The Lowdown

Core Concept

You're the new kid. Your mission: survive the social jungle of high school, forge alliances, stay off the radar of the fun police, and cap it all off by throwing the most epic party this town has ever seen.

Genre

Social Survival Sim meets Narrative RPG with a heavy dose of Comedy.

Your Turf

Key Locations

  • The Suburbs: Your home base.
  • The Heights: Where the rich kids live.
  • Northwood High: The main social battleground.
  • The "Badlands": Risky, but rewarding.
  • The Woods/Beach: Classic hangout spots.
  • The Skate Park: Grind and chill.

The Cliques

Every action has a reaction. Piss off the Jocks, and you might find an unlikely friend in the Nerds. It's all connected.

Jocks Roadmen Nerds Druggies Gamers Normals

A Day in the Life

The School Day Loop

From the morning bell to the 8 PM curfew, every moment counts. Attend class or skip? Socialize or lay low? Navigate passing periods and a risky lunchtime to shape your day.

The Social Grind

It's not just who you know, it's how you know them. Win dialogue minigames, get invited to hangouts, and don't be a social leper. The ultimate goal? Throwing legendary parties.

Hangouts & Chemistry

Get numbers, get invites. But choose your guest list wisely. Mixing rival cliques is a recipe for disaster. A good party boosts your rep, a bad one tanks it. P.S. Sharing is caring.

Dynamic Spawning

Life's unpredictable. You won't always load your save in the same spot. Wake up in bed, in a bush after a party, or in the middle of a... private moment. Keeps things fresh.

The Nitty Gritty

Cash is king. Choose your path to riches:

The Straight & Narrow:
  • Mowing Lawns: Follow the path perfectly for a better tip. Buy better mowers to work faster.
  • Washing Cars: A timed minigame to clean off all the dirt spots evenly.
  • Tutoring: If your grades are high, offer to help others with a simple math minigame for cash.
The Side Hustle:
  • Flipping Items: Use an "eBuy-like" app to find items NPCs want and sell them for a profit.
  • The Bully: Use intimidation dialogue to raise an NPC's fear meter and demand their lunch money. Fail, and you lose social standing.

Don't get caught. This is a game of cat and mouse.

Hiding Your Stash:

Your parents can search your room, and school authorities can search your locker. Disperse illicit items in various hiding spots (loose floorboards, specific boxes) to avoid getting busted and grounded.

Sobriety Checks:

Stumble home intoxicated and you might face a minigame to avoid suspicion:

  • Breathing Game: Control a meter with the spacebar to simulate steady breathing.
  • Stand Still Game: A QTE where you must quickly press W, A, S, or D.
  • Delete Emails Game: Sneak to the family PC, find the password by looking for fingerprints, and delete incriminating evidence before a parent walks in.
World Hazards:

The wrong part of town at the wrong time (like the Badlands at night) means you risk getting robbed. A higher reputation makes you less of a target.

From party starters to mind-benders, what you use and who you get it from is a core part of the social climb. Each has unique effects and risks.

  • Alcohol: Get a fake ID from a specific student to buy from stores.
  • Weed: Find a dealer and build loyalty for better prices.
  • Shrooms: Become good friends with a weed dealer.
  • Molly: Achieve a high social standing with a specific Jock to get the contact.
  • LSD & DMT: Endgame drugs unlocked by maxing out loyalty with specific, high-level dealers.
  • "Study Pills": Boosts grades but carries a high risk of addiction.

For the truly ambitious. Plan and execute heists managed through your phone's "Notes" app. The loop is simple: Unlock the heist, Recruit a skilled crew, complete Setup missions, and Execute for big rewards.

Example Heists:
  • Small Scale: Steal a teacher's wallet, change your grades on the school computer, or swipe the answers for next week's quiz.
  • Large Scale: Plan and execute a full-on raid to steal a large quantity of weed from a rival dealer's stash house.

The Ultimate Goal

Go from a nobody to a legend. Your final exam? Throwing an end-of-year party so epic, it goes down in Northwood High history.
